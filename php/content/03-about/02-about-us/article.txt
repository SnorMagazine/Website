Title: Who We Are

----

Text: We want to democratize one of the key tools, used to influence legislation. Our plan is to work towards making The Lobby a reality. Right now we are in our infancy. If you like what we are doing, or have ideas to share, drop us a line!

The Lobby was created by Nick Meehan, an American designer, for his senior thesis at The Design Academy Eindhoven, in The Netherlands.
