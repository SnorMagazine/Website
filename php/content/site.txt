Title: The Lobby

----

Author: Nick Meehan

----

Description: The Lobby's goal is to democratize one of the darkest parts of the American political system, the political lobby. It is being developed as an online platform that gives users the opportunity to create and crowdfund a political lobby.

----

Keywords: crowdfunding, political lobbying, government, money
